# AEGISOS v0.3.8
# ADR-004 — Knowledge Graph & Semantic Index

Status: Accepted

Version: v0.3.8

---

# MISSION

AEGISOS now possesses a continuously updated World Model.

The next milestone is to transform isolated information into connected knowledge.

Implement the Knowledge Graph & Semantic Index subsystem.

This subsystem is responsible for discovering, storing, indexing, and exposing semantic relationships between every entity inside AEGISOS.

It is NOT an AI reasoning engine.

It is NOT a planner.

It is NOT an autonomous system.

It is the structured knowledge foundation upon which every future planner, agent, and automation workflow will operate.

---

# DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Everything is an entity.

Everything is connected.

Knowledge should be queryable.

Relationships should be first-class citizens.

Semantic retrieval should complement—not replace—structured graph traversal.

The graph must remain deterministic, auditable, and extensible.

# FINAL OBJECTIVE

At the completion of ADR-004, AEGISOS should possess a production-grade Knowledge Graph and Semantic Index capable of representing every important entity, relationship, and semantic concept within the operating system.

The Knowledge Graph becomes the canonical knowledge substrate for Executive Planning, Multi-Agent Runtime, Memory OS, Automation Engine, and the AEGIS Cockpit.

Every future intelligent subsystem should retrieve knowledge through this layer rather than maintaining independent indexes or isolated semantic stores.
