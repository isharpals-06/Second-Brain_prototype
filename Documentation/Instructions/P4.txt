# AEGISOS v0.3.0
# ADR-003 — World Model Engine

Status: Accepted

Version: v0.3.0

---

# MISSION

AEGISOS can now perceive its environment through Sentinel.

The next step is to understand that environment.

Implement the World Model Engine.

The World Model is the living representation of the user's digital world.

It continuously receives events from Sentinel, correlates them, maintains state, and provides a unified understanding of the user's current activity.

The World Model DOES NOT reason.

The World Model DOES NOT plan.

The World Model DOES NOT execute actions.

Its only responsibility is maintaining an accurate, real-time representation of reality.

---

# DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

The World Model is the single source of truth.

Every subsystem must consume state from the World Model rather than building its own internal context.

All state should originate from observed events.

No duplicated context.

No isolated caches.

No agent-specific world state.

Everything must remain deterministic and reproducible.

# FINAL OBJECTIVE


At the completion of ADR-003, AEGISOS should possess a continuously updated, deterministic World Model representing the user's digital environment.

Every future subsystem—including the Executive Planner, Multi-Agent Runtime, Automation Engine, and AEGIS Cockpit—must rely on this World Model as their single source of truth.

The World Model should behave as the operating system's understanding of reality, while remaining independent from AI reasoning and autonomous decision-making.
