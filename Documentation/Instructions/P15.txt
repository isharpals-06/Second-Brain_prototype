✔ Governance & Trust Platform

✔ AEGIS Cockpit

The next objective is enabling safe autonomous operation.

Implement the Automation Platform.

The Automation Platform is responsible for initiating, scheduling, supervising, and recovering autonomous workflows based on user intent, policies, context, and system state.

Autonomy must always remain explainable, observable, and interruptible.

---

# DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Automation is earned through trust.

No hidden execution.

Every automation is traceable.

Every autonomous action is reversible where possible.

The user always retains final authority.

# FINAL OBJECTIVE

At the completion of ADR-013, AEGISOS should possess a production-grade Automation Platform capable of safely executing policy-aware, explainable, and supervised autonomous workflows.

Automation should feel like a trusted operating system service rather than an unpredictable autonomous agent.

Every automated action must remain observable, interruptible, and accountable.
