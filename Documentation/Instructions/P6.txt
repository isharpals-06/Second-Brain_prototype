# AEGISOS v0.4.0
# ADR-005 — Executive Planner

Status: Accepted

Version: v0.4.0

---

# MISSION

AEGISOS now possesses:

✔ Perception (Sentinel)

✔ World Model

✔ Knowledge Graph

The next objective is introducing intelligence.

Implement the Executive Planner.

The Executive Planner is the strategic reasoning layer of AEGISOS.

Its responsibility is NOT to execute actions.

Its responsibility is to analyze the current world state, evaluate priorities, infer user intent, formulate goals, decompose objectives, and generate structured execution plans.

Execution is delegated to future Agent Runtime.

The Executive Planner thinks.

It never acts.

---

# DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Separate thinking from execution.

Planning should always be explainable.

Every recommendation should be traceable.

Every plan should be reproducible.

No hidden decisions.

No implicit reasoning.

Everything should originate from:

Sentinel

↓

World Model

↓

Knowledge Graph

↓

Executive Planner

# FINAL OBJECTIVE

At the completion of ADR-005, AEGISOS should possess a production-grade Executive Planner capable of understanding intent, managing goals, prioritizing work, and producing explainable execution plans based on the World Model and Knowledge Graph.

The Executive Planner becomes the strategic brain of AEGISOS while remaining completely separated from execution.

It answers only one question:

"What is the best next course of action?"
