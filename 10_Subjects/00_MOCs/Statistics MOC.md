## 🏛️ Unit 1: Probability Foundations & Distribution Theory

This section covers the foundational probability axioms, random variables, joint distributions, tail bounds, and standard discrete/continuous distributions.

*   **Probability & Random Variables**:
    *   [[Probability Spaces and Conditional Probability]] - Kolmogorov axioms, independence, total probability, and Bayes' Theorem.
    *   [[Random Variables and Distribution Functions]] - Discrete PMF vs. continuous PDF, and Cumulative Distribution Functions (CDF).
    *   [[Expectation and Joint Distributions]] - Linear expectation, variance, covariance, correlation, Chebyshev's Inequality, and the Weak Law of Large Numbers (WLLN).
*   **Probability Distributions**:
    *   [[Common Probability Distributions]] - Reference profiles for Bernoulli, Binomial, Hypergeometric, Poisson, Uniform, Exponential, Gamma, and Normal (Gaussian) distributions.
